My Earth Store, Dharamsala

DHARAMSALA

In the lap of Himalayas, at Dhauladhar mountain ranges, the idea of My Earth Store was born in 2009. A group of close friends, who had renounced their respective high power corporate careers in big cities were on a journey of knowing the nature of reality.

During their numerous travels, meeting people, working with local communities & experimenting with Organic Farming, the group watched the environmental & community issues very closely. The group realized the need to develop a model of holistic living, which creates a beautiful harmony of life process all around & creates an inner balance & harmony. This automatically guides and energizes the process of awakening in every human being.

The first model got implemented in the hill sate of Dharamshala and is called My Earth Store ☺

Today My Earth Store works very closely with a number of NGOs, self help groups, rural & urban communities of Artisans, independent artists & a couple of socially conscious businesses. My Earth Store promotes Hand Crafted, Natural & Organic Products procured via these carefully chosen associate’s group.

Come & continue to be a part of this evolutionary process…
